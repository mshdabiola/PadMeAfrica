Donate

Your financial support allows us to purchase sanitary pads and educational materials. Every money counts! Donate securely online or send a check.

Volunteer

Share your skills and passion! We have opportunities for volunteers of all backgrounds to assist with events, fundraising, educational workshops, and administrative tasks.

Spread Awareness

et's break the silence! Talk openly about period poverty and Pad Up Africa's work. Share our stories and resources on social media using #PadUpAfrica and #EndPeriodPoverty. Organize awareness campaigns in your community or school.

Fundraise

Unleash your inner champion! Organize fundraising events like bake sales, marathons, or online campaigns. Every penny raised brings us closer to ending period poverty. We offer a fundraising toolkit with resources to help you get started.
