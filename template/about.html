About Us

Pad Up Africa: Championing Period Dignity and Education for Girls

Pad Up Africa is a non-profit organization on a mission to dismantle period poverty and empower girls across Africa. They achieve this by tackling two crucial aspects: access to menstrual hygiene supplies and education.

Period Poverty: A Silent Disruption

Many girls in Africa experience period poverty, the lack of access to sanitary pads or tampons. This reality forces them to miss school days, impacting their education and overall well-being. Pad Up Africa addresses this by distributing sanitary pads to girls in underprivileged communities. This ensures they can manage their periods hygienically and attend school with confidence.

Education is Key: Breaking the Stigma

Pad Up Africa recognizes that providing supplies is just one part of the solution. They also implement educational programs to address the stigma surrounding menstruation and promote healthy menstrual hygiene practices. By educating girls and their communities, Pad Up Africa fosters a more open and understanding environment.

Investing in the Future

Pad Up Africa's work is essential in ensuring girls' access to education, a key driver of development. Their mission empowers girls to stay in school, manage their periods with dignity, and reach their full potential. Every girl deserves the chance to learn and thrive, and Pad Up Africa is making a lasting difference.
